# Skills Matrix

Self-assessed skill levels. All scores are **initial self-assessments** and will change as evidence accumulates.

## Scoring scale

| Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 0 | Not started |
| 1 | Familiar with the term |
| 2 | Can explain it |
| 3 | Have practiced it |
| 4 | Can build with it |
| 5 | Can teach or lead it |

**Rule:** every nonzero score needs a short evidence statement. Do not award advanced scores based only on watching or discussing material — practice or building must be real.

**Note on a score of 3:** a 3 may reflect guided practice (following a course exercise) and does not by itself imply repeated, independent capability. Where the evidence comes from a guided course exercise, it is labeled as such below.
